About Benjie

Benjie Lindo is the Founder and President of Yakap Alberta Foundation, where he is dedicated to supporting newcomers and driving community initiatives in Alberta. He also serves on the Board of Nasipitnon in Edmonton, reflecting his commitment to creating positive change.

With extensive experience in Marketing Solutions, Benjie helps businesses grow through tailored brand strategies, targeted social media campaigns, and expert consulting. He specializes in digital marketing, offering workshops and webinars that provide actionable insights for startups and businesses looking to expand their reach and boost sales.

Since 2013, Benjie has been deeply involved in charitable efforts, including GoFundMe campaigns and his “Adopt a Child” program, which has provided education for 50 students in the Philippines.

Benjie’s work empowers businesses to improve their marketing strategies and helps individuals in the community lead successful, fulfilling lives. Whether you’re looking to grow your brand or make a difference in your community, Benjie offers the expertise and resources to help you succeed.
